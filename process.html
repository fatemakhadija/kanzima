The 5 Stages of Creation

1. The Khaka (Blueprint)

The design's soul is born on paper, a detailed map for the artistry to follow.

2. The Tracing

The intricate pattern is transferred onto the finest fabric using traditional methods.

3. The Adda (Mounting)

The fabric is stretched taut on a wooden frame, creating the artisan's canvas.

4. The Zardozi Stitch

Master karigars meticulously breathe life into the design with gold wires and embellishments.

5. Final Assembly

The embroidered pieces are crafted into the final, exquisite garment, ready for its royal debut.
